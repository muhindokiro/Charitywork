NGCE

Nomad Girl-Child Empowerment

Nomad Girl-Child Empowerment (NGCE) is a community-based organization, based in Marsabit County in Northern Kenya. NGCE is a non-partisan organization driven by values such as humility, trust, integrity, inclusivity, collaboration, respect, and resilience. It was founded in 2021 to eradicate child marriages, teenage pregnancies, and female genital mutilation (FGM) to restore the dignity of young girls in society.
